What is Gridley?

I needed a way to quickly merge a handful of images into a simple, single grid-like image without having to load up monster programs like PhotoShop or clicking copy/paste dozens of times in Windows Paint. While there are a handful of tools and websites out there that came close none of them did exactly what I wanted so I just made something myself ... which I've named Gridley™. Gridley™ is a very simple, but powerful, Windows application that can save a lot of time in a variety of situations such as:

  • Screenshot Consolidation
  • Creating Photo Thumbnail Sheets
  • Comparing Design Mockups
  • Documention/Tutorials Callout Graphics
  • Compressing Multiple Images into One
  • Generating Photo Collage Desktop Wallpaper
  • ... and plenty more I'm sure

Gridley™ is free so download today and see if you find it useful too!

Frequenty Asked Questions

How many images can the application process at once?

You can drag or open up to 15 images in Gridley™ at once. Please note the size and dimensions of your images may result in faster or slower performance so use your own judgement on how many images to process at once.

How many images total per grid can the application handle?

As of today the limit is 20 images max. This may go up or down in the future with upates.
